About Me – PolandLet'sPlay

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm PolandLet'sPlay – a passionate indie game developer, publisher, director, animator, and content creator from Poland 🇵🇱. I'm the founder and CEO of Engineering Softcodes™ and the creator behind projects like Insane Forest, Choice of Light, World of Ruin, and many more.

My Mission

My goal is to build unique indie games, create a game engine called Dynamic Rise, and launch platforms like Orbit Game Store to change how we play and share games – especially for people who can't afford high-end PCs.

What I'm Working On

  • 🎮 Insane Forest Alpha 2 – Survival horror in development.
  • 🚀 Dynamic Rise Game Engine – My own engine supporting 2D/3D, VR, console & mobile.
  • ☁️ Orbit Game Store – A hybrid cloud platform for indie games.
  • 📽️ YouTube Devlogs, TikToks, and Music.

Skills & Tools I Use

  • 🖥️ Engines: Unity, Unreal, IT’sMagic, Godot
  • 🎨 3D/Animation: Prisma3D, Blender
  • 🧠 Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, Lua, Python
  • 🎼 Sound: FL Studio, CapCut

Fun Facts

  • I'm 14 years old and fully self-taught 💪
  • I love survival and FPS games – inspired by Rust, Stalker 2, and Warzone.
  • I make my own soundtracks and trailers!
  • I want to help other devs and studios grow with my publishing skills.
