The Story

In the late 70s, a man developed robotic dummies for clothing stores. At first, they were revolutionary. But strange events followed—security guards disappeared, and the dummies started moving on their own. After the creator was arrested, production stopped.

Years later, a transport vehicle carrying the remaining dummies crashed in a forest. The wreckage was found—but no dummies, no driver. Locals whisper about Skinwalkers. In the 2000s, you run out of fuel near that same forest. The nightmare begins again...
