The Story

In the late 70s, a man began creating unique dummies for clothing stores—dummies that moved. They were a success until strange behaviors began: security guards vanished, reports of movement at night… Then he was arrested, and the production was shut down.

Months later, a transport vehicle went missing in a forest. The wreckage was found. No driver. No dummies. Rumors spread of Skinwalkers. Now, in the 2000s, you run out of fuel while driving at night. Trapped in the forest, the horror begins again.
